About Pokéflute

Pokéflute is a project by fancyfinn9 aiming to re-write sheet music for all the original Pokémon audio, in an easily accessable and editable way.

We plan to release all the sheet music in MuseScore 4 format.

If you want to contribute please fire us a message on Discord! (@fancyfinn9)
